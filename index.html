Hi, I'm Zsolt!

Here is an introduction about me.

I work as a user interface engineer. I have been making websites since 2005 and started developing web applications in 2018. I currently work for Oracle in a Project Lead position. I also do numerous other things.

As part of my work, one of the things I enjoy most is teaching, both one-on-one and presenting to a crowd. In 2021, I had the opportunity to deliver two lectures at a local university as part of an open course. I talked about Agile development practices and accessibility.

I often write about my thoughts on Medium. I write about general things as well as articles about web development.

I also play the drums and create educational videos on YouTube.
